# SISKOPATUH - Hajj & Umrah Information System

SISKOPATUH is a comprehensive management application designed specifically for hajj and umrah travel agencies. The system includes various modules to manage all aspects of the travel business.

## Key Features

### CRM & Leads Management
- Pilgrim data management
- Lead tracking and conversion
- Booking and reservation system

### Operations
- Travel package management
- Departure scheduling
- Document tracking (passport, visa)
- Hotel and transportation management

### Finance & Accounting
- Payment and installment system
- Financial reports
- Accounting integration
- Commission tracking

### Multi-Tenancy
- Tenant system for multiple travel agencies
- Subscription management
- Role-based access control

## Tech Stack

- **Backend**: Laravel 11 with Livewire 3
- **Frontend**: Flux UI, Tailwind CSS
- **Database**: MySQL
- **Authentication**: Laravel Fortify
- **Testing**: Pest PHP

## Challenges & Solutions

One of the biggest challenges was designing a flexible system for various types of hajj and umrah packages with complex pricing. The solution was to create a modular pricing system with support for discounts, promo codes, and configurable additional fees.

## Results

This application successfully improved the operational efficiency of hajj and umrah travel agencies, reducing booking processing time by up to 60%, and providing real-time visibility into financial and operational status.
